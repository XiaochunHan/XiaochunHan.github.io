Xiaochun Han

I'm a research postdoc in psychological and brain sciences at Dartmouth College where I work with Dr. Tor Wager.

I study the psychological and brain bases of social emotion and cognition, such as intergroup relationships, belief, and empathy, using EEG, functional MRI, and computational modeling. I seek to understand how intergroup relation influences our beliefs, emotion perception, and decision-making process via the brain and how to eliminate negative biases created by intergroup relationships.
